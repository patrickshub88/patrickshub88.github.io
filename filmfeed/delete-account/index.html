Delete Your FilmFeed Account

Last updated: 4 July 2026

You can permanently delete your FilmFeed account and all data associated with it at any time. Deletion is immediate and cannot be undone.

Delete from within the app

  1. Open FilmFeed and go to the Account tab.
  2. Choose Delete Account.
  3. Confirm. Your account is deleted immediately.

Request deletion without the app

If you no longer have the app installed, email patrick.odonovan88@gmail.com from the email address or with the phone number linked to your account, with the subject "Delete my FilmFeed account". We will verify the request and delete the account within 30 days.

What gets deleted

Deleting your account permanently removes all data linked to it, including:

  • Your profile (name, username, avatar) and sign-in identity (phone number, email, or Google identity)
  • Your reviews, ratings, and Great Movie picks
  • Your watch lists and custom lists
  • Your friendships, friend requests, and invites
  • Any contact-matching data derived from your address book

No data is retained after deletion, except records we are legally required to keep (for example, moderation reports concerning other users' safety), which are retained without your account identity where possible. See the Privacy Policy for details.

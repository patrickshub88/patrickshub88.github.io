FilmFeed Privacy Policy

Last updated: 2 July 2026

FilmFeed ("the app", "we", "us") is a movie-discovery and social app. This policy explains what personal data the app collects, why, how it is stored, and the choices you have. We collect only what the app needs to function. We do not use your data for advertising or cross-app tracking, and we do not sell your data.

Who we are

FilmFeed is operated by FilmFeed. For any privacy question or request, contact us at patrick.odonovan88@gmail.com.

Data we collect

We collect the following, all linked to your account and used solely to provide the app's features:

Data When Why
Phone number At sign-up (required) Phone-based sign-in via SMS one-time code; used to identify your account.
Email address Optional Account recovery, if you choose to add one.
Google account identity Optional Account recovery, if you choose to link Google sign-in. We receive only your Google account identifier and email.
Username / display name At sign-up Shown on your public profile and on your reviews, comments and lists.
Profile photo (avatar) Optional Shown on your profile, if you upload one.
Your content As you use the app Ratings, reviews (public by default), comments, watch lists, custom lists, favourites, reactions, the contributors you follow, and friend connections you create.
Account identifier Automatically A unique ID that ties your data to your account.

Contacts (friend discovery)

If you choose to find friends from your contacts, the app reads phone numbers from your device address book only to match them against existing FilmFeed users. Phone numbers from your contacts are sent to our server only for that matching and are not stored. We never read contact names, emails, or other contact details, and we never upload your address book. This feature is optional and only runs after you grant the contacts permission.

Notifications

With your permission, the app sends local reminders (e.g. to rate a film you recently watched) and notifications about friend activity. You can disable these in your device settings at any time.

What we do not do

  • We do not use any third-party advertising or analytics SDKs.
  • We do not track you across other apps or websites.
  • We do not sell or rent your personal data.
  • Movie data is fetched from The Movie Database (TMDB) and OMDb. We send only movie queries to these services — never your personal data.

How your data is stored and protected

Your account and content are stored on Supabase (PostgreSQL and file storage). Data is encrypted in transit using TLS. Access is enforced by row-level security so that you (and, where you choose to share, your friends) can access your data, while others cannot. Public information — such as your username, avatar, reviews and public lists — is visible to other signed-in users by design.

User content, reporting and moderation

FilmFeed lets you post public content (reviews, comments) and interact with other users. You can report objectionable content or users, and block any user so that neither of you sees the other's content. Reports are stored so we can review them and remove content or accounts that break our Community Guidelines. See our Terms of Use for the rules that apply to content you post.

How your data is shared

Within the app, content is shared with other users only according to its visibility: your profile, public reviews/comments and public lists are visible to other signed-in users; your watch list and private lists are visible only to you (and to specific friends when you explicitly share a list). We do not share your data with third parties for their own purposes.

Deleting your account and data

You can permanently delete your account at any time from Account → Account Management → Delete account inside the app. This immediately and permanently deletes your account and all associated data — your profile, phone/email on file, ratings, reviews, comments, watch list, lists, favourites, reactions, follows and friend connections. This cannot be undone. To request deletion without the app, email patrick.odonovan88@gmail.com and we will action it.

Children

FilmFeed is not directed to children. You must meet the minimum age required by your jurisdiction (and by the app stores) to create an account.

Your rights

Depending on where you live (e.g. under GDPR or CCPA), you may have the right to access, correct, export or delete your personal data, and to object to or restrict certain processing. You can exercise most of these directly in the app; for anything else, contact patrick.odonovan88@gmail.com.

Changes to this policy

We may update this policy from time to time. Material changes will be reflected by the "Last updated" date above and, where appropriate, surfaced in the app.

Contact

Questions or requests: patrick.odonovan88@gmail.com.

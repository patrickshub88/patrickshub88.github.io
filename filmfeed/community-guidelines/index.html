FilmFeed Community Guidelines

Last updated: 2 July 2026

FilmFeed is a place to share honest opinions about films and connect with friends. To keep it welcoming, everyone must follow these guidelines. They form part of our Terms of Use. We have zero tolerance for objectionable content and abusive users.

Be respectful

  • No harassment, bullying, threats, or hate speech targeting anyone based on race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability, or other protected characteristics.
  • Disagree with a film, not with people. Critique the movie, not other users.

Keep it clean and legal

  • No sexually explicit or pornographic content, gratuitous violence, or shocking material.
  • No illegal content, promotion of illegal activity, or infringement of others' intellectual property.

Be genuine

  • No spam, scams, or misleading content.
  • No impersonation of other people, brands, or FilmFeed staff.
  • Don't post other people's private information.

Reporting and blocking

If you see content or a user that breaks these guidelines: - Report it — tap the "…" menu on a review, comment, or profile and choose Report. Reports are confidential. - Block a user — from their profile (or the "…" menu on their content), choose Block. You won't see each other's content, and any friendship is removed.

Enforcement

We review reports and may remove content, or suspend or permanently remove accounts, without notice, for violations. Serious or repeat offenders will be removed from FilmFeed.
